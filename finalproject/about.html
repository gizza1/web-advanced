What is Air Pollution?

Air pollution is the contamination of the air by hramful substances such as smoke,dust,chemicals,and toxic gases.It is mainly caused by vehicle emissions,factory waste,burning fuels, and other human activities.Air pollution can have serious effect on human health,causing respiratory diseases and other health problems,while also damaging the environment and contributing to climate change.Reducing air pollution is essential for creating a healthier and safer world for future generations

What is Water Pollution?

Water pollution is the contamination of rivers,lakes,oceans, and other water sources by harmful substances such as chemicals,plastic waste,sewage systems, and industrial waste.It occurs when pollutants enter water bodies,making the water unsafe for humans,animals,and plants.Water pollution can harm aquativ life,spread diseases,and refuce the availability of clean drinking water.Protecting our water resources is essencial for maintaining healthy evosystems and ensuring a sustainable future for everyone

What is plastic pollution

Plastic pollution is a major environmental problem that harms wildlife, ecosystems, and human health. Millions of tons of plastic waste enter the environment every year, and exposure to plastic-related pollution can contribute to serious health issues. Reducing plastic use and improving recycling can help protect both people and the planet.
