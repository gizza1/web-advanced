🌬️ What is Air Pollution?

Air pollution occurs when harmful substances enter the atmosphere. These pollutants can cause respiratory diseases, heart problems, and environmental damage.

💧 What is Water Pollution?

Water pollution happens when harmful chemicals and waste contaminate rivers, lakes, and oceans, making water unsafe for humans and wildlife.

❤️ Health Effects

  • Asthma
  • Lung Cancer
  • Heart Disease
  • Respiratory Infections
  • Waterborne Diseases
